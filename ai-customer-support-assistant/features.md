Features : Features were prioritized based on impact on customer experience, operational cost, and technical feasibility.

Core (MVP):
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for grounded answers with source citations.
- Smart triage and routing to classify intent and route to appropriate queues or agents.
- Agent assist: reply suggestions, canned responses, conversation summaries.
- Knowledge base sync: connectors to documentation, ticket history, and FAQs.
- Multichannel input: chat, email, and web widget support.
- Basic analytics: volume, deflection rate, CSAT tracking.
- Rate limiting, auth, and basic logging for auditing.

Nice-to-have (post-MVP):
- Multilingual support and automatic translation.
- Fine-tuning / instruction-tuning for domain-specific accuracy.
- Adaptive prompt engineering and active learning loop.
- Conversation context windowing and long-term memory.
- Voice channel support and IVR integration.
- Automated summary reports for agents and managers.
- Human-in-the-loop review workflows and feedback capture.
